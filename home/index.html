Caring and responsive Grants Pass legal services

Daniel Simcoe provides Southern Oregon with caring and responsive legal services. He offers representation in a wide range of legal areas with an emphasis on Family Law, Personal Injury, and Criminal Law. Daniel Simcoe drafts and reviews Contracts, prepares Estate Planning documents, including Wills and Trusts, and represents clients that find themselves in a dispute, including matters that may require a court appearance.

An experienced Southern Oregon legal advocate

Whether your issue is simple or complex, Daniel Simcoe will be your strong advocate at trial, at settlement negotiations, or in hearings. To learn how Mr. Simcoe can help with any legal matter, contact his Southern Oregon law office. Daniel Simcoe has been practicing law since 1981 and is admitted to practice in Oregon and California. Mr. Simcoe offers caring representation to all of Southern Oregon, including Grants Pass, Medford, Klamath Falls and Roseburg.
